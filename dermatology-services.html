01. Minor Skin Procedures

Dermatosurgery

Dermatosurgery includes a range of minimally invasive procedures used to diagnose, remove, or treat various skin and scalp conditions. It is commonly recommended for skin tags, moles, cysts, benign skin growths, scalp lesions, and other dermatological concerns. Every procedure is carefully planned and performed in a safe clinical environment, with complete guidance for recovery and aftercare.
