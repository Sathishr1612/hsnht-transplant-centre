Facial hair plays an important role in defining facial appearance and confidence. Patchy beard growth, sparse eyebrows, genetics, previous injuries, or hormonal factors can affect natural facial hair growth.

At HSN Hair Centre, every procedure begins with a detailed consultation and facial assessment to understand your goals before recommending the most suitable restoration treatment for natural-looking results.

Expert Solutions

Advanced Facial Hair Restoration Treatments

Our facial hair restoration procedures are carefully planned to restore beard and eyebrow density while maintaining natural direction, shape, and facial harmony.

01. Facial Hair Restoration

Beard Transplant

A beard transplant is an advanced procedure designed to restore patchy, uneven, or sparse facial hair by transplanting healthy hair follicles into the beard area. Every graft is carefully placed to match the natural direction and density of existing facial hair, creating fuller and natural-looking beard growth.

Learn More
02. Precision Restoration

Eyebrow Transplant

Eyebrow transplantation restores thin, sparse, or over-plucked eyebrows using individually transplanted healthy hair follicles. Every procedure is carefully planned to recreate natural eyebrow shape, density, and symmetry while complementing the patient's facial features.
